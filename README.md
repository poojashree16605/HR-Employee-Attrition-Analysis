# HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify factors influencing employee turnover. Using Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib, I performed data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization to uncover meaningful business insights.

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook
* GitHub

## Dataset Information

* Dataset: IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset
* Records: 1470 Employees
* Features: 35 Columns

## Key Findings

* Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition rates.
* The Sales department has the highest attrition rate among all departments.
* Employees with low job satisfaction are more likely to leave the company.
* Poor work-life balance is associated with increased employee attrition.
* Employees who leave the company earn lower average salaries than employees who stay.

## Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime and workload pressure.
* Improve employee engagement and satisfaction programs.
* Focus retention efforts on departments with higher attrition rates.
* Promote work-life balance initiatives.
* Review compensation strategies for employees at risk of leaving.
